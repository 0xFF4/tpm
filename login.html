Welcome to tpm.

tpm is a vibrant platform designed to foster collaboration and innovation within the terminal developer community. At its core, tpm provides a space for developers to share and discover plugins, commands, and tools written in template languages that enhance their experiences. Central to the tpm ecosystem is the tpm cli, a powerful tool that empowers developers to seamlessly manage, install, and publish a diverse array of plugins.
